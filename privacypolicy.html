Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what data Pub Runna collects and how it’s used.

Effective date: 14 January 2026

Information we collect

Pub Runna does not require an account. The app may access your device geolocation only when you explicitly allow it to centre the map or create routes. We also use localStorage to save plans locally in your browser.

How we use data

Geolocation data is used exclusively to provide mapping and routing features; it is not transmitted to our servers by the app. Saved plans are stored in your browser's localStorage and are not uploaded anywhere unless you explicitly create a share link.

Third parties

Pub Runna uses third-party services for map tiles and routing (OpenStreetMap, OSRM, Overpass). Please review those providers' policies for more details.

Analytics

Pub Runna uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics may collect anonymised information such as pages visited, device type, and approximate location. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect usage information. This data is used solely to improve the app experience and is not used to identify individual users.

Contact & changes

If you have privacy questions or requests please open an issue on the project repository or contact the developer (see "about" page). We may update this policy occasionally; changes will be reflected on this page.
