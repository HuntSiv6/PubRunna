Welcome to Pub Runna

Here's how to start

1
Set Your Location
Click the "📍 Locate me" button to automatically find your current position using GPS. Alternatively, type an address or street name in the manual address field and click "Set location" to position the map there. You can also drag the blue marker on the map to adjust your starting point precisely.
2
Find Nearby Pubs
Set your search radius (in km) and dead-zone (minimum distance from your location). Choose between pubs or bars. Click "Find" to search for venues within your radius. The blue circle shows your max distance, red circles indicate dead-zones around selected pubs. Use "Search a pub" to find specific venues by name.
3
Generate Random Routes
For spontaneous adventures, click "Generate random route" after setting your radius and dead-zone. This creates a randomized pub crawl path, selecting pubs that meet your criteria while respecting distance constraints. Perfect for discovering new places!
4
Plan Your Crawl
Click on pub markers to add them to your "Ride-On" list. Reorder stops by using the up/down arrows, or set individual dead-zones per pub. The summary shows total trips and estimated travel time. Use "Route selected" to see the path on the map, or "Reset route" to clear it.
5
Save & Share Plans
Name your plan and click "Save" to store it locally in your browser. Load previous plans from the dropdown. Click "Share" to copy a link that others can use to view your exact route. Plans persist between sessions.
Are you ready to jump in?
Start planning your perfect pub crawl with our interactive map tools!
